    About project

    Kaveri Pebbles, a project by Biome Infra will be one of the best developments in Ananthnagar, phase II in Electronic city. Buget modern life style homes designed and developed with quality to win your trust.Simple and sensible 2BHK homes from 900sqft to 1050 sqft. Large European style Bay windows increase the use of space, light and ventilation. Kaveri Pebbles truly a place that defines homes.

    Bay windows – combination of wall with MS railings and aluminum windows. Long windows – combination of MS railings and aluminum windows. Washbasins in toilets.Kitchen sink - with water tap provision. Utility - provision of sink

Generator backup for each flat upto 1 kw / 100% for common areas. Fire resistant electrical wires.TV & telephone points for living & all bedrooms.
